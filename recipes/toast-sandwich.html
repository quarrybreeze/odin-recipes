Toast Sandwich

Description

A toast sandwich is a sandwich made with two slices of bread in which the filling is a thin slice of heavily buttered toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast a slice of bread.
  2. Butter the toast.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the toast between two slices of bread.
